NHL Player Salary Prediction

This project was designed to empower NHL executives with a data-driven approach to roster building and financial planning. A regression model designed to predict the average annual value of an NHL player's contract was trained on 10 years of player stats and salary data scraped from CapFriendly, Moneypuck, and Hockey-Reference. The model was deployed as an in-progress web app using Streamlit and Render.

Subreddit Classification with Pushshift API and NLP

Using Reddit's Pushshift API, approximately 200,000 total reddit submissions were collected from the r/hockey and r/hockeyplayers subreddits. The text submissions were cleaned and pre-processed using natural langauge processing before being fed through a stack of seven different classification models. The final modeling stack classified user submissions into the appropriate subreddit with 95% accuracy, serving as a proof-of-concept for the use of NLP as a tool for drafting text for highly targeted marketing campaigns.

Quantifying Impact of EV Charging Stations on Air Quality

The goal of this project was to evaluate the air quality impact of recent efforts to improve electric vehicle infrastructure through the expansion of available charging stations throughout the United States. Time-series modeling was used to forecast air quality levels, and a regression model was used to predict the number of charging stations required to meet a given air quality target.
